## utkr̥ṣṭatā
tatra śrīmad-aṣṭākṣarasya śēṣa-śēṣi-sarva-tattva-kaṇṭhōktyā  
‘na mantrō 'ṣṭākṣarāt paraḥ’ ityādi-pramāṇa-sahasrēṇa cācāryair viśēṣataḥ saṅgrahaḥ kriyatē ।  
itara-vyāpaka-mantrābhyām ētasya mantrasya  
atiśayēnārtha-viśēṣa-pratipādakatvaṁ mantrārtha-prakāśanēnaiva jñāpyatē ।  
viṣṇu-gāyatryām apy ētan-mantrākṣarasya prathamōpādānam  
ētat-prabhāvātiśaya-dyōtanārtham  
iti mantavyam ॥

## praṇavaḥ
ayaṁ ca mantraḥ yadā praṇavēna sākam aṣṭākṣarō bhavati।  
tadā 'sya vaidikatvaṁ traivarṇikādhikāratvaṁ ca bhavati । 

yadā praṇava-rahitatvaṁ  
tadā tāntrikatvaṁ sarva-varṇādhikāratvaṁ ca ।  

tac cōktam -

> vaidikaṁ brāhmaṇānāṁ syād  
rājñāṁ vaidika-tāntrikam ।  
tāntrikaṁ vaiśya-śūdrāṇāṁ  
sarvēṣāṁ tāntrikaṁ tu vā ॥

iti ।  
idaṁ vacanaṁ prāyikam -  
brāhmaṇa-prāśastya-paraṁ, śūdrāṇāṁ vaidikānadhikāra-paraṁ ca  
vaiśyasyāpi vaidikē 'py adhikārāt ।  
anyac cāpy atra -

> na svaraḥ praṇavō 'ṅgāni  
nāpy anya-vidhayas tathā ।  
strīṇāṁ tu śūdra-jātīnāṁ  
mantra-mātrōktir **iṣyatē** ॥

ityādi-vacanam anusandhēyam ।  

## svarāḥ
iyaṁ cātra svara-vyavasthā -

> +++(namō nārāyaṇāyētyatra)+++  
ṣaṣṭhādyayōr udāttas syāt  
svaritō 'ntya-dvitīyayōḥ ।  
pracayas tri-caturthābhyāṁ  
nihitaṁ pañcamākṣaram ॥
>
> udātta-svara ōṁkāraḥ 

iti ।  

## aṣṭākṣaratā
kathaṁ tarhi praṇava-vaidhuryē  
mantrasyāṣṭākṣaratvaṁ bhavati । ucyatē -

> tatrōttarāyaṇasyādir  
bindumān viṣṇur antataḥ ।  
bījam aṣṭākṣarasya syāt  
tēnāṣṭākṣaratā bhavēt ॥

iti ।  
idaṁ vacanaṁ praṇava-vaidhuryē 'py aṣṭākṣaratvam upapādayatīty asyārthaḥ,  
upadēśād ēvāvagamyata  
iti vivicya likhyatē । 

yadā tv ayaṁ mantraḥ praṇava-caturthī-vidhuraḥ  

## namō nārāyaṇa
tadā pratilōmānām apy adhikārō bhavati ।+++(4)+++ 

dr̥śyatē hi -

> namō nārāyaṇētyuktvā  
śvapākaḥ punar āgamat ।

tadā saṅkīrtana-mātratvam ēvēti  
na kaścid virōdhaḥ । 

## pada-trayātmakatā

> ‘ōm ity ēkākṣaraṁ,  
nama iti dvē akṣarē,  
nārāyaṇāyēti pañcākṣarāṇi’ 

ity ēvam-ādikā ।

### a
tatra akāras sarva-kāraṇa-bhūtaṁ sarva-rakṣakaṁ sarva-śēṣiṇaṁ śriyaḥ-patim āha । 

#### viṣṇu-vācakatā
tathā hi -

> samasta-śabda-mūlatvād  
akārasya svabhāvataḥ ।  
samasta-vācya-mūlatvād  
brahmaṇō 'pi svabhāvataḥ ॥  
vācya-vācaka-sambandhas  
tayōr arthāt pratīyatē ।

iti ।  
idaṁ vacanaṁ  
sarva-śabda-kāraṇa-bhūtō 'kāras  
sarva-vācaka-kāraṇa-bhūtaṁ paramātmānam āhēti vadati ।  

akārasya sarva-śabda-kāraṇatvaṁ  
sarva-vēda-mūla-bhūta-praṇava-prakr̥titvāt ।

śrūyatē hi  

> ‘yō vēdādau svaraḥ prōktō  
vēdāntē ca pratiṣṭhitaḥ +++(ōṁkāraḥ)+++।  
tasya prakr̥ti+++(→a)+++-līnasya +++(ōṁkārasya)+++  
yaḥ paras +++(vācyatayā)+++ sa mahēśvaraḥ’ 

iti ।  
atra praṇava-prakr̥tir akāraḥ ।  
tathā ca akāra-prakr̥tikatvād akāra-viśiṣṭasya praṇavasya  
yaḥ paraḥ yō vācyatayā pradhānaḥ  
**sa mahēśvaraḥ** sa nārāyaṇa  
ity arthaḥ । 

‘akārō viṣṇu-vācakaḥ’ iti niyamāt ।  

> ‘a iti bhagavatō nārāyaṇasya prathamābhidhānam  
abhidadhatā kiṁ nāma maṅgalaṁ na kr̥tam’

iti śābdikair abhidhānāc ca ।  

‘akṣarāṇām akārō 'smi’ iti bhagavad-vacanāc ca । 

kathaṁ tarhi mahēśvara-śabdaḥ prayujyatē ?  
mahān īśvarō mahēśvara iti śriyaḥ-patēr ēvābhidhānam  
iti na virōdhaḥ ।  

ata ēva 

> ‘tam īśvarāṇāṁ paramaṁ mahēśvaraṁ,  
taṁ daivatānāṁ paramaṁ ca daivatam’ 

iti jagat-kāraṇatvēna sampratipanna-nārāyaṇa-paratayā  
tatra tatra mahēśvara-śabdaḥ yujyatē ॥

ēvaṁ ca sati itarēṣāṁ tan-nāma-dhāraṇāt  
tad-guṇa-lēśa-yōgitvāt,  
tac-chēṣatvāc cēti  
sarvam upapannam ēva । 

‘akārō vai sarvā vāk’  

ity-ādīny api vacanāni  
akārasya sarva-śabda-mūlatvam āhuḥ ।  

tēna bhagavatas sarva-kāraṇatva-siddhiḥ ॥

#### nityānāṁ kāraṇam
> nanu kathaṁ nityānām īśvaraḥ kāraṇam ? 

ittham ।  
anityās tāvat anityēcchayōtpannāḥ  
nityās tāvat anityēcchayā siddhā  
iti tat-tad-dharmi-grāhaka-pramāṇair ēvāvagamyatē। +++(5)+++

tad uktam abhiyuktaiḥ -

> icchāta ēva tava viśva-padārtha-sattā  
nityaṁ priyās tava tu kēcana tē hi nityāḥ ।

iti । +++(4)+++ 

ataḥ īśvarasya sarva-kāraṇatva-siddhiḥ ।  

#### trividha-kāraṇatvam
tac ca kāraṇaṁ trividham ।  
nimittōpādāna-sahakāri-bhēdāt ।  
saṅkalpa-viśiṣṭa-vēṣēṇa nimittatvam ।  
sūkṣma-cidacid-viśiṣṭa-vēṣēṇa upādānatvam ।  
sahakāri-bhūta-kālādy-antaryāmitayā sahakāritvam  
iti vibhāgaḥ । 

#### rakṣaṇē
‘ava-rakṣaṇē’ iti dhātāv  
akāra-mātrāvaśēṣatayā vyutpannō 'yam akāras  
sarva-rakṣakaṁ śriyaḥ-patim āha । 

iyam atra vyutpattiḥ  
‘ava-rakṣaṇē’ ity asmād dhātōr  
"anyēbhyō 'pi ca dr̥śyata" iti kvip-pratyayē kr̥tē  
‘lōpō vyōr vali’ iti dhātōr vakārasya lōpē  
"kr̥tēr ēva pr̥ktasyē"ti pratyaya-vakārē ca luptē sati  
a iti vyavatiṣṭhatē।  

kr̥dantatvēna kr̥t-taddhita-samāsāś cēti prātipadika-saṁjñāyāṁ  
tādarthyē vihita-caturthy-ēka-vacanē kr̥tē  
"āya" iti bhavati ।

### luptacaturthī
> ‘brahmaṇē tvā mahasa  
ōm ityātmānaṁ yuñjīta’ 

iti praṇava-vivaraṇa-rūpē vākyē  
"brahmaṇa" iti caturthy-utpādanād

sā tu chāndasatvād atra luptēti mantavyam  
ity uktaṁ prāg ēva ।

makāra-vācyasya jīvasya  
akāra-vācya-paramātmāpēkṣayā tādarthyaṁ pratīyatē ।  

atō bhagavataś śēṣitvaṁ siddhaṁ bhavati ।  
tādarthyam ēva śēṣatvaṁ,

> ‘patiṁ viśvasya ātmēśvaram’,

vibhūtir ubhayātmikā’

#### sarva-śēṣitvam, ēkōna-sarva-śēṣitvam
> ‘ubhayādhiṣṭhānaṁ caikaṁ śēṣitvam’

asyārthaḥ -  
yathā putrāpēkṣayā mātā-pitrōr dvayōr apy ēkaṁ śēṣitvaṁ,

pitur ēva sarva-śēṣitvaṁ,  
ēvam asmad-apēkṣayā divya-dampatyōś śēṣitvam ēkam ēva ।+++(5)+++ 

paraspara-vicārē

iti ।

#### śarīrātma-bhāvaḥ
tēna śarīrātma-bhāvō 'pi siddhō bhavati ।  

> ‘yasya cētanasya yad dravyaṁ śēṣataika-svarūpaṁ  
tat tasya śarīram’ 

iti bhāṣyakārā niraṇēṣuḥ ।  
anēna cētanaṁ śarīram ēva bhavati।  
īśvaraś śarīry ēva bhavati।  
"cētanas tu śarīraṁ, śarīrī ca bhavatī"ti vibhāgō draṣṭavyaḥ ।

#### dēvatāntarādi-varjanam
anēna dēvatāntara-varjanam api siddhaṁ bhavati ।  
idaṁ ca dēvatāntara-varjanaṁ tadīya-varjana-paryantam ēva -  
tadīyānāṁ  
bhagavati sāmyādhikyādi-vipratipatti-bhūyastvēna  
atyanta-varjanīyatvāt । +++(4)+++

yathā bhagavac-chēṣatvaṁ bhāgavata-śēṣatva-paryantam upapādayiṣyatē  
tadvad idam api dēvatāntara-varjanaṁ  
tadīya-varjana-paryantam ēva ।  
atra ca -

> ananya-dēvatā-bhaktā  
yē mad-bhakta-jana-priyāḥ ।  
mām ēva śaraṇaṁ prāptās  
tē mad-bhaktāḥ prakīrtitāḥ ॥  

> brahmāṇaṁ śitikaṇṭhaṁ ca  
yāś cānyā dēvatās smr̥tāḥ ।

ētat tu guṇa-kriyādiṣv adravyēṣv api samānam।  
ittham ēva -

#### guṇāḥ
nāra-śabdēna  
samāśrita-saṁrakṣaṇōpayukta-guṇāḥ prakhyāpyantē ।

##### dayā
tēṣāṁ svarūpaṁ pradarśyatē ।  
svārtha-nirapēkṣā para-duḥkha-nirācikīrṣā **dayā** ।  
yē tu para-duḥkha-duḥkhitvaṁ dayēti varṇayanti, tan-matē  
nikhila-hēya-pratyanīkatayā nirūpita-svarūpasya bhagavatō duḥkha-prasaṅgābhāvēna  
dayā-svarūpam avōcchidyatē ।  

> "vyasanēṣu manuṣyāṇāṁ  
bhr̥śaṁ bhavati duḥkhitaḥ" 

ity-ādikaṁ  
samāśritānāṁ svasminn anurāga-viśēṣa-siddhayē  
duḥkhitvābhinaya-mātram ēvēti  
sarvācāryāṇāṁ nirvāha iti  
prācīnam ēva dayā-lakṣaṇaṁ  
bhāṣyakārābhimatam iti mantavyam ॥

"dayā 'nyēṣāṁ duḥkhāprasahanaṁ",  
"dayā para-vyasana-hā" ity-ādikam atraiva saṁvādaḥ।

**svāmitvaṁ**, sarva-śēṣitvam ।

##### vātsalyam

iti ।  
ayam arthaḥ—

iti saṅkalpa-viśēṣa-rūpā kādācitka-dayā

svabhāva-vāda-parigrahē tu

anayaiva diśā

atra caraṇa-śabdēna  
dāsasya svāmi-viṣayē caraṇa-grahaṇam ēva samucitam  
iti prakāśyatē ।  

#### aṅga-vaiśiṣṭyam
na khalu rāja-bhr̥tyā rājānaṁ kara-grahaṇēna svābhilaṣitārthaṁ prārthayantē ।  
anatilaṅghanīyaṁ hi viśēṣēṇa caraṇa-grahaṇam  
ity arthō 'tra bhāvanīyaḥ ।  
tathā ca  
"na jātu mātuś caraṇau jihāsati"  
iti yāmunācāryair abhihitam ॥

kiṁ ca mātur hr̥dyāvayavāntarēṣu satsv api  
stanandhayasya putrasya  
stanayōr upajīvyatvam ।  
ēvaṁ śaraṇāgatasya bhagavac-caraṇāravindayōr ēva bhōgyatvam  
itīmam arthaṁ prakāśayati caraṇa-śabdaḥ ।  

śrūyatē hi "viṣṇōḥ padē paramē madhv utsaḥ" iti । 

#### śaraṇam, upāyatvam
atra caraṇa-śabdēna upāyatvam abhidhīyatē ।

> upāyē gr̥ha-rakṣitrōś  
śabdaḥ śaraṇam ity ayam ।
vartatē sāmprataṁ tv ēṣa  
upāyārthaika-vācakaḥ ॥

iti vacanāt ।  

##### śrī-vaiśiṣṭya-tulyatā
atra caraṇayōr upāyatvābhidhānaṁ  
caraṇa-viśiṣṭasya bhagavata upāyatvē paryavasyatīti,  
ayaṁ ca nyāyaḥ  
+++(śrīnibha→)+++ upāya-viśēṣaṇatayōpādīyamānēṣu guṇēṣv api tulya  
iti mantavyam antēvāsibhiḥ ।  
atō 'trāpi nōpāya-dvitva-prasaṅgaḥ ।+++(5)+++  

atra nityānvayi viśēṣaṇam ।  
kādācitkatvam upalakṣaṇam -  
yathā "prakr̥ṣṭa-prakāśaś candraḥ",  
"kākavad dēvadattasya gr̥ham" iti।  
tēnātra śrīmac-chabdādy-upāttānām arthānāṁ viśēṣaṇatvam ēva -  
sarvēṣāṁ tēṣāṁ kāryānvayitvāt । 

### prapadyē
'prapadyē' iti mahā-viśvāsō 'bhidhīyatē ।  
pra-śabdēna prakarṣābhidhānāt  
"pall̥ gatau" iti dhātōr  
"gatyarthā budhyarthā" iti nyāyēna  
buddhēr abhidhānāt  
buddhēś cādhyavasāya-rūpatayā viśvāsa-paryavasānāc ca ॥

#### bharanyāsē paryavasānam
ētad uktaṁ bhavati ।  
"bhagavac-caraṇāravinda-yugalaṁ śaraṇam adhyavasya  
prārthanā-pūrvakaṁ bharaṁ nyasyāmī"ti  
nyāsasyāpi buddhi-rūpatvāt  
'prapadyē' iti nyāsa ēvātrābhidhīyatē ।  

kēvalaṁ viśvāsābhidhāna-mātrē  

> "nyāsaḥ pañcāṅga-saṁyutaḥ",  

> "ātmātmīya-bhara-nyāsō  
hy ātma-nikṣēpa ucyatē",  

> "tasmān nyāsam ēṣāṁ tapasām atiriktam āhuḥ" 

ity-ādi-pramāṇānanvayaḥ prasajyēta ।  

> "viśvāsō dvayārthaḥ" 

ity ācārya-vacanasyāpi  
bhara-nyāsa-paryanta-viśvāsē tātparyam ।  

anyathā 

> "tava bharō 'ham akāriṣi dhārmikair  
aśaraṇam ity api vācam udairiram" 

ity-ādi-vacanaṁ virudhyēta ॥

ēvaṁ ca 'prapadyē' iti śabdaḥ  
prakr̥ṣṭa-jñāna-rūpaṁ bhara-nyāsam ēva prādhānyēna prakāśayatīti kēcit ।  

tathā ca svēna vā svācāryēṇa vā  
sva-rakṣaṇa-viṣaya-bhara-nyāsaḥ kartavya  
ity uktaṁ bhavati । 

#### aṅga-kalanam
uttama-puruṣēṇa prapadana-kartr̥-puruṣa-viśēṣābhidhānāt  
adhikāra-rūpam ākiñcanyaṁ  
tat-prakāśātmakaṁ kārpaṇyābhidhānam aṅgaṁ ca pratipādyatē ।  

ataḥ prātikūlya-vacanam ānukūlya-saṅkalpaś ca  
śrī-śabdēna pratīyatē ।+++(5)+++  
mahā-viśvāsas sōpasarga-dhātau,  
tatraivārthāc chabdād vā gōptr̥tva-varaṇaṁ, bhara-nyāsaś ca sidhyati -  
gōptr̥tva-varaṇa-bhara-nyāsayōr api prakr̥ṣṭa-buddhi-viśēṣa-rūpatvāt । 

uttama-puruṣē kārpaṇyam ākiñcanyaṁ ca  
"na dharma-niṣṭha" ity-ādi-yāmunācārya-vacanēna  
tasyaiva vivaraṇāt ।  

ataḥ pūrva-khaṇḍē sādhikāra-sāṅga-prapadana-siddhiḥ । 

### śrīman-nārāyaṇataḥ śaṅkā-nāśaḥ
atra ca śrīman-nārāyaṇa-śabdābhyāṁ  
śaṅkā-pañcaka-hētavaḥ pañcāpi pratikṣipyantē । tad uktam ācāryaiḥ—

> **kartavyaṁ** sakr̥d ēva hanta kaluṣaṁ sarvaṁ tatō **naśyati**  
"brahmēśādi-sudurlabhaṁ padam api **prāpyaṁ** mayā drāg!" iti ।  
viśvāsa-pratibandhi-**cintanam** idaṁ **paryasyati nyasyatāṁ**  
raṅgādhīśa! ramā-patitva-subhagaṁ nārāyaṇatvaṁ tava ॥+++(5)+++

iti ।  
śriyaḥ-patitva-saṁvalitair bhagavad-guṇaiś śaṅkā-hētavō nirasyanta ity arthaḥ ॥

tathā hi—  
bhagavataḥ parama-kāruṇikatvāt  
prapadanōpāyasya sakr̥ttvaṁ na dōṣāya ।  
svārtha-nirapēkṣa-- para-duḥkha-nirācikīrṣā hi **kr̥pēty** abhyadhāyi ।  
**svāmitvāt** sva-śēṣa-bhūtān asmān  
svayam ēva saṁrakṣitum arhati ।  
**suśīlatvād** asman mānyaṁ nālōkayati ।  
mahatō mandais saha nīrandhra-saṁślēṣō hi sauśīlyam iti cōktam ।  
**vatsalatvād** asmad-dōṣam api na paśyati ।  
svatas sarvajñasya bhagavatō dōṣānavalōkana-vacanaṁ tad-avalōkanaṁ tan-nivāraṇāyaiva bhavatīti jñāpanārtham iti ca pūrvam ēvōktam ।+++(5)+++  
āśrita-viṣayē sulabhatvād  
**durlabhatvam** api nāsti ।  
dr̥śyatē hi 

> "ati-dūram abhaktānāṁ  
bhaktānāṁ puratas sthitam।" +++(5)+++

iti ।  
ēvam audārya-sarvajñatvādīnām api guṇānām upayōgō draṣṭavyaḥ ।  

ētē ca sarvē guṇāḥ  
śriyaḥ-patitvēnōttabhyamānāḥ  
samāśrita-saṁrakṣaṇāya prabhavantīty arthaḥ ।  

### tava
ēvaṁ pūrva-khaṇḍē trīṇi padāny anusandhēyāni ।

> śrīman-nārāyaṇa! svāmin!  
ananya-śaraṇas tava ।
caraṇau śaraṇaṁ **yātas**  
**tavaivāsmy** aham acyuta॥

iti dvaya-vivaraṇa-rūpa-- bhagavac-chāstra-vākyānurōdhēna  
śrīman-nārāyaṇa-śabdayōs sambuddhyantatvam āśritya  
'tava' iti padam adhyāhr̥tya ca  
kēcin nirvahanti । 
artha-tattvaṁ tu sarvatrāpy ēkam ēva ।  

## uttara-khaṇḍē phalam
pūrva-khaṇḍē saparikaram upāyaṁ pratipādya  
uttara-khaṇḍēna tat-phalam abhidhīyatē ।  

### śrīmatē nārāyaṇāya
tatrāpi śrīmac-chabdārthaḥ pūrvavad vyākhyēyaḥ ।  
nārāyaṇa-śabdēna guṇa-vigraha-vibhūtyādi-viśiṣṭaṁ paraṁ brahma pratipādyatē -  
kr̥tsnasyāpi bhagavad-vibhūti-bhūtasya cētanācētanātmaka-prapañcasya  
tad-viśēṣaṇatvēna bhōgyatvāt,  
nara-sambandhitayā nāra-śabda-vācyatvāc ca ।  

#### līlā-vibhūtēr bhōgyatā
yathā khalu pittōpahatasya tiktatayā bhātaṁ kṣīraṁ  
tad-apagamē madhuram ēva pratibhāti  
tathā karma-vaśāt prāg anyathā-pratipannaṁ viśvaṁ  
tan-nivr̥ttau bhagavad-vibhūtitvē sukhāyaiva bhavatīti  
kin nāma na yōyujyatē ॥+++(5)+++

ata ēva khalu  

> asaṁkucita-brahmānubhavō mōkṣa  

ity abhidhīyatē ।+++(4)+++  

### caturthī
atrāpi śrīman-nārāyaṇa-padasthayā caturthyā 'pi  
śēṣatva-puraskārēṇa kaiṅkaryam ēva pratipādyatē । 

### namaḥ
'namaḥ' iti mamakāra-niṣēdhēna  
phala-daśāyāṁ  
svādhīna-kartr̥tvaṁ, svārtha-kartr̥tvaṁ,  
svādhīna-bhōktr̥tvaṁ, svārtha-bhōktr̥tvaṁ cēti  
virōdhi-catuṣṭayaṁ niṣidhyatē । +++(4)+++ 

ayaṁ ca niṣēdhas svargādi-nyāyēna  
atrāpi tādr̥śa-dōṣam āśaṅkamānaṁ prati bōdhanāya  
iti na kaścid virōdha  
iti prāg ēvōktam । 

#### muktāv aucityam
> na khalu mōkṣa-daśāyām ētādr̥śa-dōṣāḥ pratibuddhair āśaṅkitum api śakyantē ।  
kathaṁ tarhi bhaktānām asthāna-bhaya-śaṅkādikaṁ jñāna-kāryādikam abhidhīyatē ?

maivam ।  
na hi tatra sākṣād bhayād asthāna-bhaya-śaṅkōcyatē ।  
api tv 

> asthāna-bhaya-śaṅkinām āpta-tamānāṁ kiṅkarāṇāṁ  
svāmi-viṣayē yādr̥śaḥ prēmātiśayaḥ  
stādr̥śa-prēmātiśayō muktānāṁ bhagavad-viṣaya  

ity-atō  
jaya-jayēty-ādi-vacanaṁ kiṅkara-vr̥tti-mātra-viṣayam ēvēti  
na kaścid virōdhaḥ ।+++(4)+++ 

### guṇānubhavaḥ

tasmād uttara-khaṇḍasya  
adhyāhr̥ta-niraparādha- kaiṅkarya-lābhō vākyārthaḥ ।  
bhagavadguṇānubhavaś ca tad-antargata ēva -  
bhagavad-guṇānubhava-janitatvāt kaiṅkarya-saṁtānasya ।  

## vākya-vibhāgaḥ
atra pūrva-khaṇḍē ēkaṁ vākyam ॥
uttara-khaṇḍē tu "āviḥ syuḥ" ity adhyāhr̥ta-kriyānvita- nārāyaṇa-śabda-niṣṭha- caturthī-paryantam anyad vākyaṁ kaiṅkaryasya prārthanā-rūpatvāt ।

nāpi nirapēkṣa-kartr̥tvaṁ,

ity arthaḥ ।

ēṣāṁ trayāṇāṁ vākyānām ēka-vākyatvē  
śrīman-nārāyaṇa-caraṇayōḥ sarva-dēśa-- sarva-kāla-- sarvāvasthōcita-- sarva-vidha- niraparādha-kaiṅkarya-prāpty-arthaṁ

## anuṣṭhāna-prakriyā
atrēyam anuṣṭhāna-prakriyā svānuṣṭhāna-mukhēnācāryaiḥ sunipuṇam upapādi—

> saṁsārāvarta-vēga-**praśamana**-śubha-kr̥d-(dr̥g)-dēśika-**prēkṣitō** 'haṁ  
**saṁtyaktō** 'nyair upāyair, anucita-**caritēṣv** adya **śāntābhisandhiḥ** ।  
niśśaṅkas tattva-dr̥ṣṭyā nirupadhika-dayaṁ prāpya (rthya) saṁrakṣakaṁ tvāṁ  
> **nyasya** tvat-pāda-padmē varada nija-bharaṁ, nirbharō nirbhayō **'smi** ॥

iti ।  

### anusandhānāntaram
atrāpi mūla-mantravat tattva-trayānusandhānam artha-pañcakānusandhānaṁ ca  
svayam ēva kartavyam antēvāsibhiḥ । 

siddha-sādhyōpāya-vibhāgaś ca  
pūrvam ēvōktatvād  
atra na vidhīyatē । 

### niṣṭhā
atra 

> "mūla-mantrē jātatvāt  
dvayēna saṁvardhya  
dvayaika-niṣṭhō bhavēt" 

ityādīni pūrvācārya-granthēṣu vacanāny anusandhēyāni । 

> "dvayam arthānusandhānēna saha sadaiva vaktā  
yāvac-charīra-pātam  
atraiva śrīraṅgē sukham āsva" 

itīdaṁ phala-paryavasāna-bhūmiṁ nigamayatīti  
gadyōktaṁ nigadyatē ।

## saṅgrahaḥ
lakṣmyā nitya-**samanvitaṁ**, guṇa-gaṇair **juṣṭaṁ** ca dēhānvitam  
dēvaṁ siddham upāyam **āha**, puratas **sādhyaṁ** 'prapadyē'-padāt ।  
mantrō 'yaṁ madhusūdana-praṇayinī-**yuktaṁ** punaḥ **khyāpayan** +++(phalatvēna ←"śrīmatē nārāyaṇāya")+++,  
antē +++("namaḥ" →)+++ bhōga-hr̥d-antarē mama sadā svattvaṁ na +++(kaiṅkaryaṁ→)+++ "kiṁ" kurvataḥ ॥

**śaraṇa-varaṇa-vāṇī**-tat-parāṇāṁ narāṇāṁ  
karaṇa-vilaya-kālē kalpanā-kalpa-śākhī ।  
**phalati** phalam aśēṣaṁ padmayā viśva-dhātryā  
calita-bhuja-latābhyāṁ cāru **saṁśliṣyamāṇaḥ** ॥

śaraṇāgati-mantrasya  
saṁgrahād artha-**nirṇayaḥ** ।
**vyadhāyi** varadāryēṇa  
vēdāntācārya-sūnunā ॥

iti viśvāmitra-kulōdbhūtēna nikhila-kavi-tārkika-cūḍāmaṇinā sarva-tantra-svatantrēṇa śrīmad-varada-nāthāpara-nāmnā kumāra-vēdāntācāryēṇa viracitē  
śrīmad-rahasya-traya-sārārtha-saṁgrahē  
dvayādhikārō dvitīyaḥ ॥

atha carama-ślōkādhikāraḥ॥

## svarūpam, dvaya-sambandhaḥ
ēvam anuṣṭhēyasyōpāyasya  
adhikāra-nairapēkṣyādy-- apēkṣitārtha-viśadī-kāra-mukhēna  
vidhāyakaṁ vākyaṁ carama-ślōkaḥ ।  

tatra pūrvōttarārdhābhyāṁ śaraṇāgati-mantra--pūrvōttara-khaṇḍayōr vivaraṇam iti mantavyam । +++(4)+++

## prakaraṇam
tatra karma-yōga--jñāna-yōga--bhakti-yōgān  
mōkṣa-sādhanatvēnōpadiśya  
tad-anadhikāritayā śōcataḥ phalgunasya  
upāyāntaram atra vidhīyatē । 

## sarva-dharma-parityāgaḥ - adhikāraḥ
tatas sarva-dharma-parityāga-śabda upāyāntarānadhikāraṁ pratipādayati ।  

### saṅkhyānam
tad-anadhikāraś ca pañcadaśa-vidhaḥ ।  
uktaṁ hy ācāryaiḥ—

> bhakty-ādau śakty-a-bhāvaḥ, pramiti-rahitatā, śāstrataḥ paryadāsaḥ  
kāla-kṣēpākṣamatvaṁ tv iti niyati-vaśād **āpatadbhiś** caturbhiḥ ।  
ēka-dvi-try-ādi-yōga-vyatibhidura-nijādhikriyās **saṁśrayantē**  
santaḥ śrīśaṁ sva-tantra-prapadana-vidhinā muktayē **nirviśaṅkāḥ** ॥ 

iti ।  
ayam asyārthaḥ ।  
śaktiḥ, jñānaṁ, śāstrāparyudāsatvaṁ, kāla-kṣēpākṣamatvam  
iti catvāri paraspara-saṁvalitāni bhaktēr adhikāraḥ ।  
tēṣāṁ viparyayas tu prapattēr adhikāraḥ ।  

sa viparyayaś caikaika-nivr̥ttyā caturdhā ।  
dvika-dvika-nivr̥tyā ṣōḍhā,  
trika-trika-nivr̥tyā caturdhā,  
kr̥tsna-nivr̥tyā caikaś cēti  
pañcadaśa-vidhaḥ । 

śakty-ādi-mēlana-niṣēdhasya sarvatrāpy aviśiṣṭatvāt,  

> bhakty-ādau śakty-a-bhāvaḥ, pramiti-rahitatā, śāstrataḥ paryadāsaḥ  
kāla-kṣēpākṣamatvam …

ity amīṣām ēkaika-sadbhāvē  
dvika-dvika-sadbhāvē  
trika-trika-sadbhāvē  
caturṇām api sadbhāvē  
prapattyadhikārō bhavatīty uktaṁ bhavati ।  

ēvaṁ ca, duṣkarōpāyāntarānadhikāriṇō mumukṣōs  
sukarōpāyō vidhīyata ity arthaḥ ॥

### adhikārānuvādatvam
> nanu kathaṁ 'parityajya' iti lyab-antē sati adhikārānuvādaḥ?  
itthaṁ "samāna-kartr̥kayōḥ pūrva-kāla" iti  
samāna-kartr̥katvaṁ pūrva-kāla-vartitvaṁ ca lyab-arthaḥ ।  
na tu vidhitvam anuvādatvaṁ vā ।  

tathā ca sati  
"vidhis tu dhāraṇē - apūrvatvāt" iti nyāyēna  
pratipādyamānasyārthasya pramāṇāntarāprāptatvē   
vidhitvaṁ bhavati।  
prāptē tv anuvādatvam ēva pradarśayati ।  
"aprāptē hi śāstram arthavat"  
iti hi nyāya-vidaḥ ।  

ēvaṁ ca  

> upāyāntarānadhikāriṇas tad-ananvayasya  
prāg ēva prāptatvēna  
adhikāriṇō 'nuvādaḥ kriyata 

iti na virōdhaḥ । anyathā—

> anityam asukhaṁ lōkam  
imaṁ prāpya bhajasva mām ।
trīn lōkān saṁparikramya  
tam ēva śaraṇaṁ gataḥ ।  

> laśunaṁ gr̥ñjanaṁ caiva  
bhutktvā cāndrāyaṇaṁ carēt ।

ity-ādiṣu lyabanta-pratipādyasya saṁsāra-prāptyādēr api  
vidhēyatvaṁ syāt ।  
tēnānadhikāram ēva pradarśayati,  
śaktasya cāśaktasya ca tyāga-vidhānāyōgād  
iti nīti-vidāṁ nirṇayaḥ ।  

### tyāga-vidhi-pakṣāḥ
tēna svarūpa-tyāga-vidhi-pakṣō nāṅgīkāryaḥ । 

pramāṇāviruddha-- viśiṣṭa-vidhi-pakṣa-prakāra-bhēdāś ca

### sarva-dharmān
atra sarva-dharma-śabdēna  
mōkṣōpāyatayā vihita-bhakti-yōgābhidhānam  
iti prakaraṇād avagamyatē ।  
artha-prakaraṇādīnām artha-nirṇāyakatvāt,  
tatra 'dharmān' iti bahuvacanaṁ

adhikārōpāya-phala-daśāsv anuvartatē ca ।+++(4)+++  

tasmin prapattitva-vyavahārō rājāntaraṅga-puruṣē rājatva-vyavahāravad  
iti na virōdhaḥ ।  

ēvam arthāntarēṣv api svayam ēvōhanīyam ।  
tēṣāṁ svarūpam ēva pradarśayāmaḥ ।  

adhikāri-viśēṣaṇaṁ svarga-kāma itivat phalākāṅkṣā ।  
siddhōpāya-pratipattir "īśvaraḥ siddhōpāya" iti niścayaḥ ।  

anivāraṇaṁ saṁrakṣatō bhagavataḥ prātikūlya-varjam aparādhānanuṣṭhānam ity arthaḥ ।  
tac ca prapatty-aṅgam ēva - prātikūlya-varjanātmakatvāt ।  

anumatiḥ svayam ēva rakṣaṇē pravartamānasya  
tat-prīti-saṁdhukṣaṇaṁ tad-bhara-nyāsātmakaṁ ca -  
ētat prapattir ēva ।  
na cēt tad-avinā-bhūtaṁ yat-kiṁcid-aṅgaṁ bhavatu -  
tathā 'pi ca na dōṣaḥ ।  

citta-samādhānaṁ = bhagavati svīkr̥ta-bharē nirbharatvānusandhānēna samāhita-cittatvaṁ,  
tac ca prapatti-phalam ēva ।  

caitanya-kr̥tyaṁ = svayaṁ jñātr̥tvāt sva-jñānānuguṇa-svōcita-kr̥tyānuṣṭhānam ।  

+++(īdr̥śam)+++ anyathā sambandha-jñānādy-aṅgīkārēṇa  
bhara-nyāsa-rūpa-prapatti-svarūpa-nirākaraṇē  
bahu-pramāṇa-virōdhaḥ, sat-sampradāya-virōdhaḥ, vidhi-vaiyarthyaṁ, mūla-mantrādy-artha-vibhāga-virōdhō, nyāya-virōdhaś ca prasajyēta ॥

ēvam 'a-cid-vyāvr̥tti-mātram' ity-ādi-śabdānām arthō nirvāhyaḥ ।  
naitē hy arthās sākṣāt prapatti-svarūpam ।  
tathā 'pi prapatty-anuṣṭhātr̥̄ṇāṁ sambhavati ।  
ētāvataitēṣu prapatti-vyavahāra  
iti na saṅkaṭaṁ kiṁcit -  
aupacārika-vyavahārasya sarvatra sulabhatvāt ।  

tathā ca "nyāsa iti brahma" ity-ādikam api  
siddhōpāya-prādhānyēna brahmaṇi pratipattyartham  
aupacārikam ēva ।  
na khalu siddha-sādhyōpāyayōḥ svarūpaikyaṁ sambhavati ॥+++(4)+++

brahma-vidām ēvārcirādi-gamana-pūrvakaṁ brahma-prāptir  
ity upapāditam ।  

dahara-vidyāyām api prajāpati-vākyē  
jīva--para-brahma-prāpti-rūpa— phala-nirdēśō  
jīvānusandhānasya brahma-vidyā-śēṣatvēnaivēti  
bhāṣyakārair abhyadhāyi ।  

### prakr̥ti-maṇḍalē hi
tad yathā -  

brahma-vidyā-phalatvēna upasaṁhr̥tam iti +++(kāraṇēna)+++,  
tēna jñāna-yōgasya brahma-vidyāṅgatvē  
arcirādi-mārgēṇa parama-pada-prāpty-ādikam asti ।  
karma-jñānayōr aṅgi-phalēnaivāṅgānāṁ phalavattvam ।  

svātantryē, punaḥ prakr̥ti-maṇḍala ēva  
yatra kutrāpy avasthānē  
jīvātmānubhavaḥ phalam ।  
tad ēvōcyatē bhagavatā parāśarēṇāpi— 

> "yōginām amr̥taṁ sthānaṁ  
svātma-santōṣa-kāriṇām" 

iti ।  
atra sthānasya +amr̥tatva-vacanaṁ  
cira-kālāvasthāna-param ॥

athavā jīva-svarūpa-nityatva-param ।  
tadā tu "sthānam" iti ca svarūpam ēvōcyatē ।  

### brahma-vidyāyām ēvārcirādi-mārgaḥ
brahma-vidyāyām ēvārcirādi-mārgēṇa parama-pada-prāptir  
ity ētad api  
tēnaiva parāśarēṇa viśadam uktam—

> ēkāntinas sadā brahma-  
dhyāyinō yōginō hi yē ।  
tēṣāṁ tat paramaṁ sthānaṁ  
yad vai paśyanti sūrayaḥ ॥

iti ।  

upāya-daśāyāṁ 'rāmānujam' itivad brahma-śarīra-bhūta-svātmānusandhāyinaḥ,  
'lakṣmaṇa-pūrvajam' iti svātma-śarīraka-paramātmānusandhāyinō vā  
brahma-dhyāyina ēva  
phala-daśāyāṁ tv  
asaṁkucita-brahma-jñāna-rūpatvāt  
sadā brahmānubhava-siddhiḥ । +++(4)+++ 

pūrvōktasya kēvalasya tu nēti vibhāgaḥ ।  

### asthiratā
tasyāpi tat-phalāntē 

> "sarvē cyavana-dharmāṇaḥ  
pratibuddhas tu mōkṣa-bhāk" 

iti cyavana-dharmatvam ucyatē ॥

tathā ca "pūrva-karma-viśēṣāt  
punar viṣayānubhavō 'pi ca ~~na~~ sambhavati"  
iti cyavana-dharmatvaṁ yujyatē । 

bhāgadhēya-paripāka-viśēṣa-vaśāt  
punar bhagavad-upāsanam asti cēt  
phalatayā 'rcirādinā bhagavat-prāptir  
iti tattva-vidāṁ nirṇayaḥ । 

#### viparīta-vākya-nirvāhaḥ
tāvatā tasyāpy apunarāvarti-vacanaṁ yujyatāṁ nāma - kō dōṣaḥ?  
tēna purāṇēṣu, tad-vyākhyānēṣv ācārya-granthēṣu  
jñāna-yōga-niṣṭhasyārcirādi-gamanāniṣēdhō yady asti  
tat tu bhakti-yōga-dvārēṇaivēti nirṇētavyam ।  

na punar bhakti-yōgānaṅga-bhūta-- jñāna-yōga-niṣṭhasya  
sākṣād bhagavat-prāptir arcirādi-gamanaṁ ca bhavati ।  

### yuktiḥ
yukti-virōdhas tu—  

> "parama-padaṁ gatvā  
bhagavantaṁ vihāya  
svātmānam ēvānubhavati" 

iti kaiścid abhidhīyamānaḥ  
kiṁ sarva-karma-vinirmuktaḥ,  
uta karma-vaśa ēva vā?  
antyē saṁsāry ēva bhavati, na tu muktaḥ ।  

sarva-dharma-vinirmuktatvē,  
punaḥ svābhāvikasya +asaṁkucita-brahmānubhavasya saṅkōcābhāvād,  
brahmānubhava-vicchēda ēva na syāt । 

#### na svēcchayā bhagavat-tiraskāraḥ
> "svācchandyād ēva  
para-brahmānubhavaṁ muktvāv atiṣṭhatē kēvala" 

iti hāsyam ētat ॥

#### nēśvarēcchayā
sarva-karma-vinirmuktasya para-brahmānubhava-vicchēda-rūpa-- mahānartha-pravr̥tty-asambhavāt  

> īśvara ēka ēva tasmin viṣayē nigr̥hṇāti,  
tēna tad-anubhava-vicchēda 

iti durvacam ।  
īśvarō niṣkāraṇam ēva kutracin nigr̥hṇāti cēt  
tasya nirhētuka-nigrahas syāt ।+++(5)+++  
sa tu mahān dōṣaḥ । 

sa-kāraṇaṁ cēt - karmaiva hi kāraṇaṁ,  
tan-nivr̥ttam ēvēti prāg ēvōktam ॥

tad-ēka-dēśānuvartanē  
muktir ēva na bhavēd  
ity-ādi-dōṣa-sahasrēṇa duṣṭam ēva । 

### nigamanam
tasmād yaḥ paramaikāntī  
bhakti-prapattyōr anyatarōpāyēna bhagavantaṁ prasādyāvatiṣṭhatē  
sa ēva hi siddhōpāya-niṣṭhaḥ,  
sa cārcirādi-mārgēṇa parama-padaṁ prāpnōti,  
bhagavantaṁ prāpya,  
bhagavad-anusāribhiḥ kaiṅkarya-kalāpais sarvatō vilasatīti samañjasam ētat ॥

> vimala-mati-viśēṣād vīkṣya hēyān pumarthān  
> sthira-vinihita-buddhir yas tu kaivalya-muktaḥ ।  
> punar api ca bhavēt sā saṁsr̥tis tasya kālē
> hari-bhajana-vipākān muktir ēvātha vā syāt ॥

## Goals dhyēyāni
Dyugangā is a work group dedicated to the promotion of ever-victorious Hindu ideals and arts. It's current focus is in presenting important texts for easy study. Long term goal (https://rebrand.ly/dg-archive) is to save texts to last for millennia into the coming post-electronic/ industrial age.

The texts may be presented as 

- audio files (eg: [MahAbhArata audio book project](https://sanskrit.github.io/groups/dyuganga/projects/audio/mbh-audio/)), 
- as web pages (eg. [Apastamba-gRhya-sUtra](https://vishvasa.github.io/vedAH_yajuH/taittirIyam/sUtram/ApastambaH/gRhyam/sUtra-TIkAH/), [Apastamba-dharma-sUtra](https://vishvasa.github.io/vedAH_yajuH/taittirIyam/sUtram/ApastambaH/dharma-sUtram/sarva-prastutiH/), [EkAgnikANDa commentary](https://vishvasa.github.io/vedAH_yajuH/taittirIyam/sUtram/ApastambaH/gRhyam/ekAgnikANDam/sarva-prastutiH/), [manu-smRti](https://vishvasa.github.io/kalpAntaram/smRtiH/manuH/sarva-prastutiH/05/), [raghuvaMsha](https://vishvasa.github.io/kAvyam/laxyam/padyam/kAlidAsaH/raghuvaMsham/sarva-prastutiH/01/), more [kalpa-texts](https://sanskrit.github.io/groups/dyuganga//projects/text/proofreading/kalpa-texts/), [tattva-texts](https://sanskrit.github.io/groups/dyuganga//projects/text/proofreading/tattva-texts/), [universal subhAShita DB](https://subhashita.github.io/saMskRtam/padyam/random/)),
